Our Story

Namasthe, welcome to Bombay Masala. A small shop located at the corner of Botanical Road and Featherston Street has become everything to us. Over the years we have won hearts of great people of palmy with our tasty and authentic Indian food.

The owner of Bombay Masala has worked in the food industry for over 25 years in some of the world-renowned restaurants and hotels. He started this shop in the hopes of providing the true authentic flavour of India to Kiwis.

We have over 100 dishes on our menu from street food of Bombay (now known as Mumbai) to some of the popular dishes from Southern India. We take pride in our food and we love serving you wonderful people. Here at BOMBAY MASALA, we are constantly striving to improve our service and quality in order to give our customers the very best experience.

We would like to thank everyone who supported us over the years and became part of our small family.
